The "id" refers to the level meaning id 1 = level 1

        
The "speed" refers to the speed the sleigh will go

        
The "houses" refers to the list of houses in that level

        
The "size" refers to the size of the house and there are 3 options (small, medium, large)

        
The "position" refers to the space/distance of the next house. The small house is 3 wide meaning if you
            make the position 1 the next house will be one third of the small house's size away. The medium house is 4
